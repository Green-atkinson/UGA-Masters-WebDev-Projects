Artist: SZA

Album Review: Ctrl


Ctrl which was released June 9, 2017, is the debut album by the American singer SZA.

The first song I've ever interacted with from this album is Love Galor (feat. Travis Scott) on October 23, 2020 (according to Spotify from when I added it to my playlist). Since then, I added the song Drew Barrymore on December 5, 2021, and Broken Clocks on December 22, 2021.

Recently, I was enjoying the absolutle phenomenal music that SZA makes with a friend, and they pointed out to me that I never ACTUALLY listened to the full album. As soon as they pointed that out, and I mean QUICKLY, I listened to the rest of her Ctrl album. Because the album came out in 2017, I have heard multiple of her songs around on the radio, in shows, on TikToc, and so on. Nevertheless, I enjoyed every second of each song, and really enjoy this album that SZA put out.

My favorite songs from this album are: Love Galore (feat. Travis Scott), Drew Barrymore, Broken Clocks, and Normal Girl.
